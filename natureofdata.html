The Nature of Data

Through a course called The Nature of Data, I worked on a semester-long research project looking at the various ways the Katahdin Woods and Waters National Monument (KWW) was portrayed in the media. KWW was designated a national monument in 2016 by President Obama on land set aside in Northern Maine by Roxanne Quimby, the founder of Burtâ€™s Bees.

I, along with two collaborators, looked at the differences in attitude towards the monument between Northern Maine, Southern Maine, and on a national scale as as portrayed in the media. We completed a number of different quantitative and qualitative analyses, using text analysis, correspondence analysis, and GIS mapping to aid in our research.
