Roya Moussapour

Hi! I'm Roya. I'm a 2017 Bowdoin grad working in Boston, passionate about education, technology, data visualizations, and music. I currently work at Compass Lexecon as a data analyst, spending most of my time working in our airlines practice and geeking out about Tableau.

At Bowdoin College, I majored in physics and minored in education. I was concertmaster of the Bowdoin Chamber Orchestra and business director of Miscellania, Bowdoin's oldest all-women's a cappella group.

I spent a semester abroad in Madrid studying engineering at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid, where I blogged about my experience traveling (eating) my way through Europe. I spent my academic summers at Bowdoin, in Huntsville, AL working for NASA on satellite design, and in D.C. at Bates White Economic Consulting.

I'm originally from Westchester County, NY, but have been thrilled to make New England my home for the last 5 years (and counting!). I've found an incredible community of like-minded musicians here in the Me2/Orchestra, and other than the occasional travel bug, I'm aiming to put down stronger roots in New England for the foreseeable future.
